# Profile Verification and Review Markers

Picom markers communicate a narrowly defined review action, not reputation or guaranteed trust.

- `profile_reviewed`: Picom staff reviewed stated profile ownership signals; not legal identity verification.
- `community_official`: Picom staff reviewed the relationship between a community and its stated operator; not a safety or quality endorsement.
- `role_managed`: Picom staff reviewed role-management metadata; not proof that every role holder is trustworthy.

Only app admins can grant or revoke markers. Every change requires a bounded reason and appends an immutable audit fact. Public consumers receive only active label, kind, scope note, and grant time. Internal reasons, grantor identity, and revoked history are not included in public profile projection.

There is no paid verification, automatic reputation score, engagement-based trust claim, identity-document upload, or purchasable badge in this task.
