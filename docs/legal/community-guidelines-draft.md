# Picom Community Guidelines - Draft

> Closed-beta placeholder requiring moderation and legal review. It is not final policy text.

## Respect and safety

Do not harass, stalk, threaten, exploit, dox, or target people based on protected characteristics. Do not encourage violence, self-harm, or serious abuse.

## Prohibited content

Do not distribute illegal content, child sexual abuse material, non-consensual intimate material, malware, scams, stolen credentials, or instructions intended to facilitate serious harm or unauthorized access.

## Spam and manipulation

Do not flood channels, automate unwanted messages, evade rate limits, manipulate engagement, impersonate others, or coordinate deceptive activity.

## Privacy and consent

Respect private channels, personal information, voice conversations, and screen shares. Do not record, redistribute, or expose private material without appropriate consent and authority.

## Reports and moderation

Users may report harmful content or behavior through the beta report/support flow. Authorized moderators may remove content, restrict participation, or escalate serious cases. Final appeal, emergency, and law-enforcement processes remain placeholders.
