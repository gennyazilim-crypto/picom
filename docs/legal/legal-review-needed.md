# Picom Legal Review Needed

The beta legal documents are product placeholders, not final legal advice.

Before production launch, qualified counsel should review:

- applicable entity name, contact details, and governing law;
- age requirements and consent;
- privacy legal bases, retention, user rights, subprocessors, and international transfers;
- user-content licensing and intellectual-property reporting;
- moderation, appeals, termination, and law-enforcement handling;
- beta warranties, liability limits, indemnity, and dispute process;
- voice, screen-share, attachment, and private-channel disclosures;
- accessibility and localization of binding acceptance language;
- durable evidence for terms version and `terms_accepted_at`.

No production team should present these placeholders as approved policy text.
