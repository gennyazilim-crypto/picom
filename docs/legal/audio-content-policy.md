# Picom Audio Content Policy

Document status: **Engineering draft - legal approval required**  
Draft version: `audio-content-draft-2026-07-10`

Users may upload, publish, stream, or share radio/podcast/audio material only when they own the content or have all required licenses and permissions. Picom does not grant music, performance, synchronization, broadcast, trademark, publicity, or third-party content rights.

Prohibited content includes unauthorized copyrighted recordings, unlawful content, deceptive ownership claims, malware, harassment, and content that violates the Community Guidelines or Acceptable Use Policy. Reporting and takedown requests must use the approved support/copyright process when published.

Voice rooms and screen sharing are live communication features. Recording is not enabled by default and must not occur without applicable notice, consent, and law. Picom does not claim end-to-end encryption for production voice or screen sharing.

Retention, moderation, appeals, repeat-infringer handling, jurisdiction, age eligibility, and copyright-agent details require qualified legal review before public stable distribution.
