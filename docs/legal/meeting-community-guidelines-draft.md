# Meeting Community Guidelines Draft

**DRAFT - NOT LEGALLY APPROVED**

These guidelines are an engineering draft for community-safety and legal review.

## Expected behavior

- Respect room rules, host instructions, and participant consent.
- Activate microphone, camera, screen sharing, and captions only for legitimate participation.
- Share only content you are authorized to display.
- Use captions as an accessibility aid, not as an authoritative transcript.
- Report abuse through Picom's supported reporting controls.

## Not allowed

- Harassment, threats, hate, targeted disruption, or impersonation.
- Non-consensual surveillance, recording, redistribution, or doxxing.
- Credential theft, malware, deceptive links, or malicious screen sharing.
- Attempts to bypass waiting rooms, invitations, room locks, moderation, or RLS.
- Disclosure of private meeting content outside its intended audience.

## Moderation

Authorized meeting hosts and community moderators may mute, remove, deny admission, restrict screen sharing, lock/end rooms, and preserve safe audit evidence. Community role, meeting role, presence, and account verification are separate concepts.

## Review blockers

Safety operations and authorized counsel must approve enforcement language, escalation, appeals, regional obligations, and publication/versioning before release.
