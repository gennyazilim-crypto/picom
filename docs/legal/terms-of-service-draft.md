# Picom Terms of Service - Draft

> Placeholder for product planning and closed beta review. This is not legal advice, is not final, and requires qualified legal review before public launch.

## Accounts and eligibility

Users must provide accurate account information, protect access to their sessions, and meet the future minimum-age and consent requirements approved for their jurisdiction.

## User-generated content

Users remain responsible for messages, media, profiles, community content, and other material they submit. Final licensing, takedown, appeal, and content-preservation language requires legal review.

## Safety and acceptable use

Harassment, credible threats, hate, spam, scams, malware, illegal activity, unauthorized access, impersonation, and infringement are prohibited. The [Acceptable Use Policy](acceptable-use-policy-draft.md) and [Community Guidelines](community-guidelines-draft.md) provide additional beta rules.

## Moderation

Picom and authorized community moderators may restrict content or accounts to protect users, enforce policy, satisfy legal obligations, or preserve service integrity. Final notice, appeal, evidence, and jurisdiction-specific requirements remain under review.

## Service changes and termination

Beta functionality may change, degrade, or be withdrawn. Final warranty, liability, dispute, termination, governing-law, and jurisdiction provisions must be approved by counsel before public release.
