# Picom Privacy Policy - Beta Placeholder

> Product planning draft only. This is not legal advice and requires professional legal review before production release.

## Data categories

Picom may process account data, profile data, community membership, channels, messages, attachments, settings, reports, and safety metadata needed to provide the service.

## Voice and screen share

Connection and session metadata, plus media needed for a live session, may be processed by the voice provider. Picom does not claim production end-to-end encryption in this beta.

## Logs and diagnostics

Redacted diagnostics may be exported only through explicit support actions. Passwords, auth tokens, authorization headers, and private message content are excluded from diagnostic exports by design.

## Providers

Supabase is the planned provider for authentication, Postgres, storage, and realtime. LiveKit is the planned voice and screen-share provider. Final subprocessor, transfer, and regional storage details require review.

## User rights and retention

Data export and account deletion foundations are placeholders. Final retention periods and jurisdiction-specific access, correction, deletion, objection, portability, and appeal rights require legal review.
