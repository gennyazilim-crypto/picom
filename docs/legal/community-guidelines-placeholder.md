# Picom Community Guidelines - Beta Placeholder

> Product planning draft only. Moderation and legal review are required before production release.

## Harassment, hate, and violence

Harassment, targeted abuse, hate, credible threats, glorification of violence, and encouragement of harm are not acceptable.

## Spam and illegal content

Do not distribute spam, scams, malware, illegal content, or instructions intended to facilitate serious harm or unauthorized access.

## Privacy and personal data

Do not expose personal data without permission, dox people, or share private channel material outside its intended audience.

## Impersonation

Do not impersonate people or organizations, or falsely claim affiliation, authority, or verified status.

## Reporting and moderation

Use reporting tools for safety concerns. Moderators should act proportionately, preserve audit integrity, and avoid exposing private content beyond permitted review context.

## Community owner responsibilities

Owners must publish understandable rules, appoint trusted moderators, protect private channels, respond to reports, and avoid granting permissions beyond operational need.
