# Meeting Terms Draft

**DRAFT - NOT LEGALLY APPROVED**

This document is an engineering draft for authorized legal review. It is not a published agreement.

## Service scope

Picom meetings provide real-time audio, video, screen sharing, text chat, waiting-room controls, optional live captions, and meeting history within the permissions exposed by a community. The Full MVP does not provide a meeting recording feature and does not claim end-to-end encryption.

## User choices and permissions

Users must explicitly activate microphone, camera, screen sharing, and captions. Device and operating-system permission decisions remain under the user's control. Users must not capture or distribute another person's content without a lawful basis and any required consent.

## Host and moderator powers

Authorized hosts and moderators may admit or deny waiting users, mute or remove participants, control screen sharing, lock or end rooms, and assign meeting roles. These actions may be recorded as restricted audit evidence. Role labels do not override database authorization.

## Captions

Captions require explicit room consent and may use a configured third-party speech provider. Caption output can be incomplete or inaccurate and must not be treated as an authoritative record. Transcript text is not persisted by this Full MVP.

## Prohibited conduct

Users must not use meetings for harassment, unlawful surveillance, impersonation, credential theft, malicious screen sharing, illegal content, or attempts to bypass room access controls.

## Availability and provider processing

Real-time media depends on network conditions, operating-system permissions, LiveKit, and configured providers. Service interruption and quality degradation remain possible. Provider data processing and regional transfer terms require final legal review.

## Required approval

Authorized counsel must approve jurisdiction, age requirements, consumer rights, acceptable-use language, limitation language, provider disclosures, and effective-date/versioning before publication.
