# Picom Legal Review Notes

These notes identify unresolved legal questions. They are not legal advice and must not be presented as compliance confirmation.

## Required review before public launch

- GDPR roles, lawful bases, transparency, data-subject rights, processors, international transfers, breach handling, and retention
- EU Digital Services Act applicability, notice-and-action, statements of reasons, appeals, transparency reporting, and trusted-flagger obligations
- Minimum age, parental consent, age assurance, and child-safety requirements by launch region
- Copyright notice/takedown and counter-notice process, including whether a DMCA agent/process is applicable
- Governing law, jurisdiction, dispute resolution, consumer rights, warranty, liability, and termination terms
- Moderation authority, evidence preservation, emergency disclosure, appeals, and law-enforcement request handling
- Data export, account deletion, backups, audit integrity, legal holds, and anonymization
- Accessibility, localization, platform-store, voice recording, screen sharing, and biometric/sensitive-data considerations

## Launch boundary

The current documents support product design and a controlled beta only. Qualified counsel must approve policy text, versions, effective dates, contacts, consent evidence, and regional launch conditions before a public release.
