# Picom Acceptable Use Policy - Draft

> Product and safety placeholder for closed beta. Professional legal and trust-and-safety review is required before public launch.

## Permitted use

Use Picom for lawful community communication, collaboration, events, text chat, voice rooms, and screen sharing while respecting permissions and participant consent.

## Prohibited activity

- Harassment, threats, hate, exploitation, or coordinated abuse
- Spam, scams, deceptive impersonation, or engagement manipulation
- Malware, credential theft, unauthorized access, or security bypass attempts
- Illegal content or infringement of copyright and other rights
- Attempts to access private communities, channels, attachments, or sessions without authorization
- Misuse of voice or screen sharing to expose private, harmful, or unlawful content

## Enforcement and reporting

Picom may rate-limit, suspend, remove, preserve, or review content/accounts when reasonably necessary for beta safety and service integrity. Users can report issues through the in-app feedback/reporting path. Final notice, appeal, evidence retention, and enforcement standards require review.
