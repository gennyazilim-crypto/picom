# Picom Privacy Policy - Draft

> Placeholder for product planning and closed beta review. This is not a final privacy notice or claim of legal compliance.

## Data categories

Picom may process account/profile data, community membership, roles, channels, messages, attachments, settings, safety reports, device/platform diagnostics, and authentication/session metadata needed to provide the beta service.

## Purposes and access

Data is used to authenticate users, deliver community chat, enforce permissions, operate realtime/voice features, investigate abuse, and provide support. Private content must remain limited by membership, channel permissions, backend policy, and Supabase RLS.

## Sharing and processors

Supabase and LiveKit are planned MVP service providers. Final subprocessors, international transfer mechanisms, legal bases, and region details require legal review and an approved production inventory.

## Retention and security

Retention periods are placeholders. Picom should minimize data, redact logs, avoid storing secrets in diagnostics, and document message, attachment, account, audit, and backup retention separately before enforcement.

## User rights

Account deletion and data export are product placeholders. Access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, and appeal handling must be finalized for applicable jurisdictions before public launch.

## Contact and updates

Privacy contact, controller identity, effective date, policy version, and change-notification process remain pending legal review.
